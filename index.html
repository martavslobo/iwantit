Why Shillington?

Because this place is a creative hub like no other. I am so impressed with all the graduates' work and I'd love to be able to say that I too trained there. I can't wait to get my hands on all the software, all the experimentation, making things, creating things, responding to briefs and finally build my portfolio.

But it's not just the 'what' is the 'how'. I'm in my 30's and retraining at this age means that you need to factor in multiple things. Being able to kick-start my careeer and knowing what I'll be able to achieve is really something to aspire to.

What does creativity mean to me?

I didn't know until recently. Or I knew but hadn't fully realised. It actually is who I am. It's not just what I thrive on, is what defines me a person. And that's why I realised that I can't spend the rest of my life doing any odd job. It needs to be channeled into the right one: the one of graphic design.

What do I hope to achieve?

I want to go from being a creative person to becoming a graphic designer; to build a portfolio and be able to show a body of work that speaks for itself; to come out at the other end with a new profession that suits who I am.
