Why do I want to study at Shillington?

It's a creative hub like no other. I am so impressed with all the graduates' work and I'd love to be able to say that I too trained there.

I can't wait to get my hand on all the software, all the experiementation, making things, creating things, responsing to briefs and finally build my portfolio.

But it's not just the 'what' is the 'how'.

I'm in my 30's and retraining at this age means that you need to factor in boring things like not having an income during a period of time, or taking a paycut that I may not recover for a number of years

Being able to kick-starting my careeer though and knowing what I'll be able to achieve is what really attracts me to it.

What does creativity mean to you?

I didn't know until recently. Or I knew but hadn't fully realised. It actually is who I am. It's not just I strive on is what defines me a person. And that's why I realised that I can't spend the rest of my life doing any odd job. It needs to be channeled into the right one: the one of graphic design.

What do you want to get out of your Shillington experience?

I want to go from being a creative person to becoming a graphic designer; to build a portfolio and be able to show a body of work that speaks for itself; to come out at the other end with a new profession that suits who I am.
